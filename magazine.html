Pantene Shampoo

  • Admin
  • 2012-02-21
  • 12

Pantene was first introduced in Europe in 1945, and is now the world's most popular hair care brand. The brand's origins date back to Swiss scientists who discovered pro-vitamin B5 (panthenol) while treating skin wounds with a vitamin concoction. They noticed that the hair around the wounds became stronger and healthier, and Pantene has included pro-vitamin B5 in every formula since.
